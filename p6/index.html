Welcome to the Seattle Space Needle

Here Is The Observation Deck

This vertical icon of the city is so kitschy it has become cool, and it gives a great view of the city from the top. The Observation deck is a 41 second ride to the top. As you head up, Seattle reveals its green and blue glory beneath you. Once on deck, take time to soak in the spectacular 360 degree view of Emeral City.

SKY CITY Restaurant

The world does revolve around you. Experience revolutionary dining with fresh view of seattle paired with every course. Just save room for dessert because it is to die for!

The bottom: The heart of Seattle

Down here you can experience Music. Farmer's Market. Mountain. Seaplanes. Look around and get an insider's perspective on what to see, taste and do!
