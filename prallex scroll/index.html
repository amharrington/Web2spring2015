The Greatest Love Affair

Seattle's love affair is to the coffee bean. This highly caffinated city is not only home to the originla Starbucks; but is also home to some of the best coffee houses ever developed. Follow us through a "Seattle Cup Crawl" as we explore the best Cups O' Joe Seattle has to offer... no wonder everyone is sleepless!
